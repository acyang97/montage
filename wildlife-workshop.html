WILDLIFE
PHOTOGRAPHY

In collaboration with Birds, Insects n Creatures of Asia Group (BICA), Camera Rental Centre, Srishti Digilife. There would be an online as well as a practical segment for you to apply the skills you learnt. Both segments will touch upon both bird and macro photography. (Macro photography is about photographing much smaller wildlife like insects and flowers)

WORKSHOP DETAILS

Online Workshop:
Date: 22 May 2021
Time: 7pm - 9pm
Venue: Online via Zoom

Practical Workshop:
Date: 31 May 2021
Time: 8am - 10am
Venue:
Dairy Farm Nature Park - Bird Photography
Botanic Garden - Macro Photography

INSTRUCTORS

Peter Lee

Peter Lee started his photography journey in 1987. 2011 marked the beginning of his journey in macro photography, and he became a macro photographer instructor in 2013.

Jeremiah Loei

Jeremiah Loei is an avid Bird Watcher who works in Canon Singapore. He started a Nature Watchers/Appreciation Group on FaceBook in 2012 called BICA-Birds, Insects N Creatures of Asia with 24K followers.

Daryl Yeo

Since young, Daryl has been intrigued with animals and their behaviour, so it was only natural he got hooked onto wildlife photography. He spends his time capturing images of native and migratory birds in SIngapore, but also regularly travels to places like Alaska and Africa. Daryl is currently the President of the Nature Photographic Society of Singapore.

Lim Kim Chuah

Kim Chuah started birdwatching in the late 1970s while in his teens. He has written several books, papers and articles on birds in Singapore and this region. In his spare time, Kim Chuah loves traveling to different parts of the world in search of birds.

James Wong

James started his photographic journey in the 90's with his first SLR camera. He started gotten serious about Birds photography only in 2017, with a primary interest in birds behaviors such as hunting, feeding and fights.

James Gan

James hopes to bring forth more beautiful birds, wildlife and Nature images on to the social media platform, for the world to love and care for them. He believes that the next generation and future ones need to ensure a fair balance to protect Mother Earth 🌍.
