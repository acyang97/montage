Peter Lee Instagram

Peter Lee started his photography journey in 1987. 2011 marked the beginning of his journey in macro photography, and he became a macro photographer instructor in 2013.

Jeremiah Loei Instagram

Jeremiah Loei is an avid Bird Watcher who works in Canon Singapore. He started a Nature Watchers/Appreciation Group on FaceBook in 2012 called BICA-Birds, Insects N Creatures of Asia with 24K followers.

Daryl Yeo Instagram

Since young, Daryl have been intrigued with animals and their behaviour, so it became natural when he got hooked into wildlife photography. Particularly, Daryl do not simply document, but hope to convey a story of animal behaviour and the ecosystem they live in. When in Singapore, Daryl spends time capturing images of native and migratory birds. On regular basis, Daryl travels to places as remote as Alaska and Africa to pursue his passion for wildlife photography. Daryl is also currently the President of Nature Photographic Society of Singapore.
