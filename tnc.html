    12. The Competition


  12. The Competition is open for entry from 8th May 2021 00:00 hours (Singapore Time) to 27th June 2021 23:59 hours (Singapore Time) (the “Competition Period”). Participants are required to register by filling in the registration form in the Website as stated above, and select the category(ies) they would like to join (either Open Category or Youth Category or both). The Competition Period is subject to change without prior notice at the absolute discretion of the Organiser.

  13. Any entry submitted before the Competition Period or after the close of the Competition Period shall be void and deemed as invalid by the Organiser and the Sponsors.

  14. To participate, all Participants are required to complete the following during the Competition Period:
    1. Take one to five photographs that represent ‘Change’ from the Participant’s point of view, and in accordance to the prompt (stated on our website).
      1. Images must be original photographs captured by the Participant. For the avoidance of doubt, artwork and illustrations will not be accepted.

      2. Entries with multiple photographs must have a demonstrable storyline/message going from one photo to the next. If the multiple photographs submitted are a collection of standalone pictures, only take the first photo and the caption will be taken into consideration. Determination of whether your post fits the “storyline/message” criteria will be at the sole discretion of the judging panel.

      3. Competition categories are as follows:
        1. #NUSPSChangeYouth: Youth Category, between 13 and 25 years old as of 1st January 2021

        2. #NUSPSChangeOpen: Open Category, above 13 years old

    2. All the Images are uploaded via Instagram and Participants must ensure that:
      1. their Instagram profile is set to "public";

      2. they "follow" @NUSPS, @CathayPhoto and @srishtidigilife_sg on Instagram;

      3. they tag the Images with #NUSPSChangeYouth or #NUSPSChangeOpen along with #NUSPS, #Montage2021, #CathayPhoto, #Profoto, #CreateWithSrishti, #nycsg, #ycmsg, and #youngchangemakers.

      4. Each entry has only one overarching caption no more than 50 words that explains how the Images capture the essence of their story based on their own perspective.

    3. Submissions are only valid if they satisfy the bare minimum following conditions:
      1. Participant has registered on the Montage 2021 website.

      2. Participant follows NUSPS, Cathay Photo and Srishti Digilife on Instagram

      3. Relevant hashtags are used

      4. Both the photographs and accompanying caption must align to designated theme

    4. Other information to take note of:
      1. No submission fees are required

      2. Each participant is allowed to submit for both categories if they meet the relevant age requirements

      3. Each participant can only submit one entry per category

      4. Must be residing in SINGAPORE during the duration of the event

    3. the Images submitted should be taken legally and in compliance with Singapore Law;

    4. no digital manipulation of the Images are permitted except for colour correction, the addition of filters and cropping, under agreeable amounts to be determined at the discretion of the Judges.

    5. Participants must submit unique entries for each category. The same photo submission cannot be used to enter both categories

    6. The correct hashtag must be used (i.e. either #NUSPSChangeYouth or #NUSPSChangeOpen). Each photograph can only be tagged with one category. Do not use both hashtags in one Instagram entry. Such entries will be counted towards the Open Category by default.
