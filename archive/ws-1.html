ABOUT THE WORKSHOP
A professional commercial photographer by trade, Geoff's extensive experience covers everything from fashion to automotive photography. If you’ve wondered about what it took to create his iconic shots, join us on this journey as Geoff shares his experience, behind-the-scenes and learn first-hand what it takes to make it as a professional commercial photographer in Singapore.

ABOUT GEOFF ANG
With over 20 years’ of experience in the field, Geoff, an established TVC director and the co-founder of Raw, has a celebrated career as a fashion photographer and is now a well-known regional commercial photographer at Geoffstudio.

Geoff once said that photography is not just about the photograph but also the journey and the experience that encompasses it. His mission is to create a story in every shot he takes. Geoff is also actively involved in creating photography workshops, seminars, and mentorships such as 2.0, and is passionate about sharing his experiences with people interested in photography.
