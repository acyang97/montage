ADVANCED BIRDING
PHOTOGRAPHY

Want to learn more about birding photography? As part of Montage 2021, we have invited Birding Photographer James Gan to give Montage 2021 participants an advanced birding workshop!

WORKSHOP DETAILS

Online Workshop:
Date: 12 June 2021
Time: 7pm - 8:30pm
Venue: Online via Zoom (Zoom link will be sent to participants closer to the date)

Note:
1. This workshop is only open to registered Montage Participants.
2. Signups will close 2 days before the workshop date, at 10 June 12pm.

INSTRUCTORS

James Gan

James hopes to bring forth more beautiful birds, wildlife and Nature images on to the social media platform, for the world to love and care for them. He believes that the next generation and future ones need to ensure a fair balance to protect Mother Earth üåç.
